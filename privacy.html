Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Your privacy matters deeply to us. Aurasphere was built from the ground up to respect your data, your time, and your autonomy.

What we collect: Only your email address—used solely to create your account and manage your subscription. We don't ask for your name, phone number, or any sensitive details. Payment processing is handled securely by Paddle; we never see your card information.

How your data is used:

  • CRM Dashboard: All your business data—contacts, invoices, expenses—stays on your device or in your private database. Nothing is sent to our servers.
  • AuraPost: Every piece of content you generate happens **entirely on your device**. Your prompts, drafts, and posts are never transmitted, stored, or analyzed by us.

We **do not use** cookies, analytics, advertising trackers, or third-party monitoring tools—ever.

Your rights: Under GDPR, you can request a copy of your data, correct inaccuracies, or delete your account at any time by emailing us.
